Anthony DeMeo - Founder and Mason

About Me

I started working in masonry as a teenager. The art of stone masonry brings the driving force behind why I show up every day. My mission is to bring the highest quality work to each of my clients. I grew up in Pennsylvania and enjoyed restoring historic buildings which has allowed me to hone my craft to restoring historic homes in the Bozeman area. In addition, I am well versed in new construction including brick, block, and stone. My true passion is creating art with natural building stone. I enjoy the challenge of a customer giving me a photo or idea and bringing that dream to a reality. I am grateful for the opportunity to service Bozeman and surrounding areas and desire to build a quality centered masonry team.

Education

After graduating high school, I attended the Pennsylvania College of Technology to learn all things masonry. This has allowed me to branch out into all the different facets of masonry.

How did I end up in Montana?

My family has always loved the mountains. We started making trips out west when I was a boy. I said from a very early age that one day I would move to Montana. For many years I worked in Philadelphia and surroundings counties and found myself yearning for a different way of living. The opportunity presented itself to follow my dream and I did not hesitate to go for it. The opportunity to work for an established masonry company in the valley was imperative. I was surprised to discover how much I had to learn. They were incredible mentors and I am forever grateful for their guidance. My goals of starting my own stone masonry business was calling. The ability to merge my experience from the east into the new found knowledge of the west continues to allow me to service my customers with anything they desire.

What are your goals?

My philosophy is to provide Bozeman and surrounding areas with the highest quality of masonry work. I am professional, prompt and reliable and will do everything in my power to provide top notch services to each and every one of my customers.

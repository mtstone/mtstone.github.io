About Us

Anthony DeMeo - Founder

About Me

I'm Anthony, the owner of Montana Stone Masonry, LLC. I was born and raised in Pennsylvania. My dad was and is still one of the hardest working stonemasons I know. He instilled in me the worth of working hard and providing the best work possible for his customers. I watched him build a successful business and knew one day I would hope to do the same. I started laboring for him as a teenager and was gradually taught the tricks of the trade that his uncles had once taught him.

Education

After graduating high school, I attended the Pennsylvania College of Technology to learn all things masonry. This has allowed me to branch out into all the different facets of masonry.

How did I end up in Montana?

My whole family has always loved the mountains. We started making trips out west when I was a boy. I said from a very early age that one day I would move to Montana. For many years I worked in Philadelphia and surroundings counties and found myself yearning for a different way of living. The mountains were calling. The opportunity presented itself to follow my dream and I did not hesitate to go for it. I started to work for Scott Koelzer Masonry in the spring of 2015. The opportunity to work for such an established company was amazing. I was surprised to discover how much I had to learn. This territory has a completely different stone outlook from what I was accustom to back east. They were and are still incredible mentors and I am forever grateful for their guidance.

Why open your own business in Montana?

As mentioned earlier, I watched my dad build a business from nothing, I have always had the respect of someone who could succeed in the way he did. My own desire to do so too is abundant especially now that I have started my own family. Maybe we're crazy? All I know is I want to give my family the best I can and feel this is the best way to achieve that dream.

What are your goals?

My goal is to provide Bozeman and surrounding areas with the highest quality of masonry work. I am professional, prompt and reliable and will do everything in my power to provide top notch services to each and every one of my customers.
